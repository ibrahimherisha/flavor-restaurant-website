Our Story

About Us

Since 2010, Flavor has been a sanctuary for those who appreciate the art of grilling. Our chef, Marco Bellini, brings over 20 years of culinary expertise from the finest kitchens across Italy and New York.

Every dish tells a story — from hand-selected prime cuts aged to perfection, to house-made pastas crafted with imported Italian flour. We believe great food starts with great ingredients, and every plate is a testament to that philosophy.
